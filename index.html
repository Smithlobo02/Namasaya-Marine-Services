About Us

Based in Mumbai, Namasaya Marine Services provides reliable and professional ship repair services across Indian ports and international locations. Backed by qualified Service Engineers, Mechanics, and Certified Welders, we ensure the highest standards of workmanship and safety.

Our team is available 24/7, all around the year, to minimize vessel downtime.

Our Specialized Services

Engine Services

Main engine unit jobs and Diesel Aux Engine full overhauling, including crankshaft and engine block replacement.

Fabrication

All types of fabrication work, including pipeline and plate repair or replacement.

Maintenance

Tank cleaning (hydro blasting), chipping, painting, and overhauling of pumps, valves, and electric motors.

Manpower

Specialized riding teams for sealing jobs and qualified technicians with STCW courses and Indian seaman book.

⚓ Shafting & Alignment Specialists

Namasaya Marine Services is renowned for its unmatched shafting work. We have successfully completed crankshaft replacement, engine block assembly, precision alignment, and load testing on numerous vessels across Indian and international ports.
